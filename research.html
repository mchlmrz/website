2. I supervised a student which implemented the scenario I came up with, as a therapy.


Design of protocol for testing the neuroprosthesis and experiments
Considering the neuroprosthesis developed in my thesis and subsequently improved:

1. Designed a complete experimental protocol to test the sensory-feedback
 a. what experiments to run
 b. what measurements to take
 c. what conditions to test
 d. how many stimulation channels to use
 e. what type of modulation to use (linear/exp., amp/freq/pw)
2. Run experiments with 3 patients, which included, every day of testing:
 a. Mounting the system
 b. Calibrating the electrical stimulation
 c. Problem solving in case of issues
 f. Decision taking in case of uncertainties
 d. Organizing and scheduling different groups that were providing us support for measures
3. Analyze data
